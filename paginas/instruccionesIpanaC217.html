Check-in instructions

1️⃣ Upon entering the building, check in at reception by presenting your ID. 2️⃣ Apartment Access 🔹 Take the elevator to the 2nd floor. 🔹 Upon exiting, turn left and follow the hallway until you reach Apartment C217. 🔹 On the door, you'll find a digital lock. 🔐 Code: 2255# 3️⃣ Inside the apartment, you'll find: 🔹 An electronic card to activate the energy service ⚡ (insert it in the slot on the switch upon entering). 🔹 A TAG for access to the building. 4️⃣ Access and Parking 🚗 🔹 Building Entrance: Look for the TAG sensor next to the main door 👉 swipe your key fob and... that's it! 🔹 Exit: Just wave your hand in front of the sensor ✋ and the door will open automatically (yes, just like magic). 🔹 Parking: Follow the arrows in the video 📹 to get there without any hassle.
