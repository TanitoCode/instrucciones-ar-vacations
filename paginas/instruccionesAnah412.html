Welcome to ANAH 412

Your entry to the Caribbean starts here. 🌴

In the heart of Playa del Carmen, within ANAH, an apartment awaits you that combines style, comfort, and exclusivity. A high-rise retreat, designed for you to experience the Riviera Maya without complications and with everything within reach. 📍 Address: 20 Avenida Norte esq. 14 Bis, Centro, Gonzalo Guerrero, 77710 Playa del Carmen, Q.R.

Instrucciones de ingreso

1️⃣ Registro en recepción En recepción se te entregará la tarjeta de acceso al depa. Úsala para entrar y disfrutar de tu refugio caribeño. ✨ 👉 Si vienes en coche, registra primero el vehículo para poder usar el estacionamiento techado. 2️⃣ Recorrido hasta el departamento Desde recepción, gira a la derecha y entra por la puerta de aluminio. Camina derecho y vuelve a girar a la derecha en el acceso marcado como “SUR”. 3️⃣ Sube al elevador hasta el 4º piso. Al salir, gira a la derecha y al fondo a la izquierda encontrarás el Departamento 412 Sur.

Check-in instructions

1️⃣ Check-in at reception At reception, you will be given your access card to the apartment. Use it to enter and enjoy your Caribbean retreat. ✨ 👉 If you are arriving by car, please register your vehicle first to use the covered parking. 2️⃣ Getting to the apartment From reception, turn right and enter through the aluminum door. Walk straight ahead and turn right again at the entrance marked "SOUTH". 3️⃣ Take the elevator to the 4th floor. Upon exiting, turn right, and at the far left, you will find Apartment 412 South.
