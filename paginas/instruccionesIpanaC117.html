Instrucciones de ingreso

Check-in instructions

1️⃣ Check-in at reception 🔹Upon entering the building, go to the front desk and check in using your name. 2️⃣ Access to the apartment 🔹Take the elevator to the 1st floor. 🔹When you exit the elevator, turn right until you find Apartment C117. 🔹Next to the door, you will find a MASTERLOCK security box. 🔐 Code: 2925 Inside, you will find the apartment keys and a building access tag. 3️⃣ Access and parking 🚗 🔹Entering the building: use the access tag reader next to the main door 👉 simply hold the key fob near it. 🔹Exiting: wave your hand in front of the sensor ✋ and the door will open automatically. 🔹Parking: watch the video 📹 for easy and hassle-free directions.
